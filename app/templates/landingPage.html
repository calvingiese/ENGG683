Welcome to Canada

ImmiGreat is a Software Subscription company that focuses on supporting immigrants in their transition to life in Canada. Moving to a new country already presents many challenges but using our software will make the process of calling it home much easier. We offer features that help people make connections with others who have experienced the immigration process, support videos documentation for unfamiliar items like getting a driver’s license. Every feature is intended to make the move to Canada as stress-free as possible and is based on first-hand experiences of Canadian immigrants.
